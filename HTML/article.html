About kishkinda university

The Kishkinda University will impart State of the Art education through strong theoretical and practical training to students in the area of Engineering & Technology, Commerce, Management, Humanities, Science & Pure Arts and other allied fields for the advancement of the knowledge. To institute degrees, Diploma certificates and academic qualifications on the basis of successful completion of Academic work through multiple modes of assessment. To promote qualification and Skill based education system with strong emphasis on developing the competency. To undertake programs for development and training a faculty and research scholars of the University. Create new knowledge through cutting edge technology and research. University will provide strong platform for inclusiveness and collaboration by following ethical and responsible practices with academia, industry and non-profit organizations. Foster entrepreneurship and develop sustainable solutions for the benefit of global society. To establish campuses and have study centres, constituent colleges and regional centres at various locations in Karnataka after a period of five years as per the norms of University Grants Commission (UGC) and State Government. To provide an innovative system of university level education, flexible and open with regard to methods and practices of learning, combination of courses, eligibility for enrolment, age of entry, conduct of examination and operation of programmes with a view of promote access and equity in higher learning and to encourage excellence in the new fields of knowledge. To promote National integration and the integrated development of human personality among all its stakeholders. To pursue any objective as may be appropriate and approved by the Board of Governor’s for the enhancement of the education and other related endeavours. The University shall employ a broad range of strategies to achieve its vision and objectives by expanding the horizon of world knowledge, provide instruction, teaching and learning including writing and reading, training, research and development at various levels such as elementary education, secondary education, higher education in the subject disciplines such as: Engineering and Technology comprising of Computer Science, Information Technology, Data Science, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Electronics and Communications, Electrical and Electronics, Mechanical, Civil, Computer Applications, IoT, Sensors, Network & Cyber Security, Cloud Computing, Environmental and Sustainable Engineering & in other emerging areas from time to time aligned with the industry requirements. Commerce & Management comprising of Business Administration, Business Management, Hospitality & Hospital Management, Hotel Management, Tourism Management, Entrepreneurship, Business Analytics, Logistics and Supply Chain, Marketing, Finance, Company Secretary, Human Resource and other emerging areas of study including the inter disciplinary areas. Humanities comprising of Economics, History, Political Science, Public Administration, Anthropology, Sociology, English, Kannada, & Foreign Languages. Fine Arts comprising of Liberal Arts, Performing Arts and other allied and emerging areas. Pure Sciences comprising of Psychology, Health Administration, Social Science, Physics, Chemistry, Maths, Biology, Statistics, quantum computations, computer science and other allied and emerging areas. Any other emerging areas of Faculty or field of study across the globe.


infrastructure

Kishkinda University will be functioning in two campuses. B.Tech, MBA & MCA programs will function inside BITM campus and BCA will function at Ballari Business College campus for two years. Subsequently, the University will be housed in a modern infrastructure that houses the administration departments of the university, and spacious classrooms and labs. These are spread over 50 acres campus namely Mountview campus near Sindhigeri, Siruguppa Road, Ballari. Ramps and lifts have been thoughtfully provided to enable easy access and movement for the differently abled and those in need. We are developing a 4 lakh Sq. ft. campus in the next 24 months that is modern and eco-friendly. The classrooms are equipped with multimedia projectors and high-speed broadband internet facilities for seamless conduct of classes. The academic block has secured Wi-Fi connectivity.
